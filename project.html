Take Me As I Am

With this piece I tried to let my emotions just guide where my hand wanted to go. It was an attempt to try and heal and let go of the feelings that I carry inside. The colours are slightly inspired by the chakras. The lock in the troat area signifies hardships with speaking my truth and that I sometimes feel an acctual block saying my piece. The eye inside the gut symbolizes the third eye, mostly located on the forhead, but I feel that our gut feeling is the closest we can come to that spiritual concept.
